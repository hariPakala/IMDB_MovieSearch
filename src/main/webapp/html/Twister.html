A daredevil exercise both behind and in front of the camera, "Twister" asks its audience to share the adventures of hot young tornado-busting scientists who thrill at the sight of each new funnel cloud. They are desperate to get as close as possible to raging tornadoes, all for the sake of meteorological research. Science aside, it's not hard to feel that these excitable characters are a wee bit berserk. But the movie works as escapism even if you do know enough to come in out of the rain. Torna
